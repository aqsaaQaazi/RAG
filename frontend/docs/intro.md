# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook! This comprehensive guide covers the essential concepts and principles of physical AI with a focus on humanoid robotics.

## What You'll Learn

This textbook is structured into 10 comprehensive chapters covering:

1. [Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics](./01-introduction) - Basic concepts and overview
2. [Foundations of Physical AI](./02-foundations-of-physical-ai) - Core principles and disciplines
3. [Humanoid Robot Design](./03-humanoid-robot-design) - Design principles and architectures
4. [Locomotion and Control](./04-locomotion-and-control) - Movement and control systems
5. [Sensing and Perception](./05-sensing-and-perception) - Sensor systems and data processing
6. [Learning Algorithms](./06-learning-algorithms) - Machine learning in physical systems
7. [Hardware Integration](./07-hardware-integration) - Integrating hardware components
8. [Ethics and Safety](./08-ethics-and-safety) - Safety and ethical considerations
9. [Future Directions](./09-future-directions) - Emerging trends and future developments
10. [Conclusion](./10-conclusion) - Summary and next steps

Each chapter includes Key Ideas and Practical Checklists to reinforce your learning.
