# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook! This book is designed to provide you with a comprehensive introduction to the fascinating field that combines artificial intelligence with physical systems, particularly in the context of humanoid robots.

## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI refers to the branch of artificial intelligence that focuses on the interaction between AI systems and the physical world. Unlike traditional AI that operates primarily in digital spaces, Physical AI deals with:

- Real-world perception through sensors
- Physical manipulation and control
- Dynamic interaction with environments
- Embodied cognition and learning through physical experience

## Humanoid Robotics

Humanoid robots are robots that resemble the human body structure. They typically have:

- A head with sensory systems
- A torso
- Two arms and two legs
- Human-like proportions

These robots are designed to operate in human environments and interact with human tools and interfaces.

## Key Ideas

- Physical AI bridges the gap between digital intelligence and physical interaction
- Humanoid robots offer unique advantages in human-centered environments
- The field combines robotics, AI, machine learning, and cognitive science
- Safety and ethical considerations are paramount in physical AI systems
